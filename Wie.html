What is IEEE NIEC WIE?

WIE Student Branch Affinity group of IEEE- NIEC is the beholder of enthusiastic aspiring women , who believe in their vision ,to transit technology through Voluntary participation and leadership roles.Here in WIE NIEC , we believe in creating a atmosphere for sustaining technological innovations through the nurturing talents of girl members that are profoundly supported by boys . Established in 2015 , WIE Affinity NIEC has emerged from being a droplet of water to a pearl of women empowerment , that is valued in terms of it's highly successful events and achievements at global level. We not only do inspirational work for technology , but allow it to become aspiration for others.This line is made into a fact by Affinity's most prominent event -STAR Program.Organised in two higher secondary girls schools , for fostering STEM education in girl students,it has stood by its name-Student -Teacher & Research Engineer/Scientist Program.

MISSION AND VISION

To be the leading provider of technical information, community services, and personalized services to the world's computing professionals.

Goals

IEEE NIEC WIE Affinity group is a dream that will continue to evolve by bringing forth women in engineering to take technology to it's Zenith.
