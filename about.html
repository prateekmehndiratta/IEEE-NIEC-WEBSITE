What is IEEE?

IEEE is the world’s largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

Why join IEEE?

IEEE's core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. IEEE will be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions.

About IEEE NIEC

IEEE NIEC is a student branch under IEEE Region 10(Asia-Pacific) in IEEE Delhi Section. It is a community of enthusiastic people who volunteers to accomplish IEEE NIEC's mission, i.e. "To utilise different IEEE resources to accomplish it's vision". Vision of IEEE NIEC is "To maintain Technical environment in campus and to involve every student and teacher technically active".
